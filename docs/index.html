Bio

I am a Data Scientist with a background in Computer Science and a desire toward building products. What keeps me engaged in this field is its multi-modality; I enjoy the challenge of adapting to different domains, whether that means analyzing sleep patterns via EEG, interpreting music mood through Deep Learning, or engineering RAG systems for dynamic narratives.

Currently, I serve as a Task Leader for EU-funded research projects, where I implement Deep Learning algorithms with a focus on Explainable AI (xAI). While this work has honed my technical rigor—culminating in a workshop presentation in Bologna—my professional focus is shifting beyond the academic sphere.

I find the most satisfaction in the engineering side of Data Science: designing the architecture, handling the pipeline, and delivering a tangible, functional MVP. I am looking for opportunities to bring this end-to-end skillset to a product-focused environment.
